### The challenge: 
Consists of a mix of all the other previous challenges. But this time,
In a more free mode version.

### What you'll do:
Your task is to help a family, connecting their devices to the internet.
The modem is preconfigured, and it's directly connected to a cloud (we will consider this the internet).

### Good tips:
Don't forget to configure IPv4, subnetmask and most likely the Default gateway as it'll be set to 0.0.0.0
Also, for my solution, i chose to secure the router with a WPA2, adding a strong password and the static IP address to it.

Finally, you may consider using a DHCP. Just know that I too considered it, but we're not gonna do that!
