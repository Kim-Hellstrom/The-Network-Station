# Geometry Bonanza!
### Difficulty ⭐⭐
Not all networks are created equal — and that's exactly the point.

In this challenge, i want you to put on your thinkie thinkie hats, because we will be going over network topology designs.
There are many out there, however i want to introduce the most common ones.

### What's in store:
The four core network topologies and their characteristics
Understanding pros and cons of each design
IPv4 addressing within a /24 network
Applying topology knowledge to a real world scenario

If you happen to get stuck, easiest method is to do a quick google search on the topology name and you'll be able to find quick
information regarding each design. Their purpose and what they look like.
And that is just it, it may seem simple and direct. 
However as you go,
I hope you'll start to ask billions of questions for more information.
Which is a great point to this challenge. 
Many times, we only recieve a limited amount of information when we need to design
large scale network designs. And so i hope, as you go through each topology, that you also start to dare making assumptions, even when
they're outlandish or wild. It helps boost our creativity, and find optimizations in our designs as they expands.

### Food for thought:
Why does each topology exist?
When would you choose one over another?
What happens if a cable or device fails in each design?

### How to navigate:
There are four stations to complete, each representing a different topology. Follow the compass labels to navigate between them. 
When all four are done there is one final creative task waiting for you at the bottom left of the workspace. 
Make sure to complete the other ones before jumping into the final task
