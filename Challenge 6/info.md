# TierGod
Difficulty: ⭐⭐


Starting on the next block, we reintroduce the concept of topology, this time in considering things like Single points of failure.
There is many ways to strategise a solution to a common problem.
I hope with this one, that you'll start to connect the dots when it comes in network design,
Convenience, cost effectiveness and robustness all has a place in an effective redundancy topology.

### The challenge: 
To create a robust and realistic network for a home business to operate their business on.

### What you'll do:
Your task is to design a topology that is suitable for a SOHO network, so that when maintanance is required,
that the business and its employees can safely use the network without too much interference.

### Good tips:
Consider reading up on Tier 1, 2 and 3 networks. As well as on multilayer switches that operates in both layer 2 and 3.
It can help in your descision when choosing what and where to connect devices.

All the devices have been preconfigured, however if you decide to add other clients or routers, it is necessary to also
configure their network IP and subnetmask, and as usual, don't forget the default gateway then.

Goodluck!
