# The Network Station 📡 

### Hello and welcome! 👋

Thank you so much for taking the time to check this out. I'm a Swedish-born individual who loves connecting ideas and solutions together — hence why networking seems so fascinating to me.

I hope you'll find the challenges here equally interesting. As you work through each case, you'll not only get to put your troubleshooting skills to the test, but also get a small glimpse into my developing understanding of IT Networking.

This repository is a great resource for anyone looking for practical, hands-on experience using Cisco Packet Tracer — and hopefully a fun one too! The challenges are designed to question your understanding of different networking concepts, so rather than just rushing to fix the issue, take your time, ask questions, and explore the why behind each problem. Reach out to me or anyone in the community — network with people! 🤝


### How to Play 🎮


Prerequisites: This assumes you have Cisco Packet Tracer correctly installed on your device. No prior networking experience is needed for the first challenges — beginners are very welcome!



Before downloading anything, read the .md file that matches the name of the challenge folder. It'll give you a sense of what concepts are covered, any tips, and a difficulty rating (note: difficulty is subjective and based on my personal assessment at the time of writing).

When you're ready, follow these steps:


📥 Download the raw .zip file <br />
📂 Extract the folder <br />
🔓 Open unsolved.pkt to begin the challenge <br />
🏁 Open solved.pkt to compare your solution when you're done <br />



### Stuck? 💡 
No worries — there are numbered screenshots inside the folder to guide you through my solution. Start from 1.png and work forward so you don't accidentally spoil anything for yourself!
