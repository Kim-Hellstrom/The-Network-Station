# Captain Planet Would Be Proud
### Difficulty ⭐⭐
One person's trash is another person's treasure.

In this challenge, you have been assigned a position to assist a Non-profit organisation in designing their HQ network. 
They are very grateful for any help you may assist with, and hopes to make the upgrade not too expensive. 
They recently retrieved donations from locals in the form of hardware for the network and struggle to make anything work. 
Seems like it's the modules missing.

### What's in store:
Understanding hardware modules and their purpose
Selecting the correct module for each device
IPv4 addressing and subnet masking within a /24 network
Default gateway configuration
Basic troubleshooting of physical and configuration issues

### Food for thought:
Why does the correct module matter for a given cable type?
What happens when a device doesn't know where to send traffic outside its network?
Is there a subtle detail somewhere that's easy to overlook?

### How to navigate:
The organisation has three rooms. Each room has its own network. 
Your job is to get every device connected, configured and communicating. 
Don't forget, the routers have already been preconfigured for you. 
Focus on the devices.

# Tip: 
Make sure the default gateway is set correctly.


Every bit of help counts. Even in networking. 🌱
