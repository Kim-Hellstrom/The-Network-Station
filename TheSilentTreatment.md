# The Silent Treatment

Please feel free to try it out! This is a beginner friendly Lab challenge.

Challenge:
It's a very simple exercise were PC1 is having troubles connecting to PC2 despite having a physical connection.

Info:
This will test basic knowledge on media interfaces, ARP and 
the TCP/IP & OSI model:
Layer 1 [physical]
Layer 2[data-link]
If anything seems confusing I highly recommend checking these concepts out. There are tons of great content on Youtube explaining them.
